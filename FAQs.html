Frequently Asked Questions

Sometimes people ask us things. Here's the most common ones.

1. How do you decide what's ethical?

This is the big one. If there was a simple answer to this there would probably be a lot less suffering and environmental destruction in the world. There are many great organisations out there who research companies and products and grade their ethicality. It's a huge, never-ending task, and it's very hard to do well. That is not what Balu is about.

Instead, Balu aims to create better choice for you, the consumer, by sharing great brands and products that you may otherwise not hear about. Ultimately, the choice is yours - as is the responsibility.

The big brands that command the marketing - the ones that float to the top of every internet search or take prime spot on the supermarket shelves - are very often not the brands putting the most effort into improving their industries. On the flip side, there are thousands of small organisations - social enterprises, charities, companies - that genuinely go the extra mile. We think it's only fair they get a look in on our purchasing decisions.

So how do we choose which ones make it into Balu? Well, mostly we don't. As a member of the Balu community, you can tell us about your own favourite brands. You suggest a brand or product, we add it to the Balu database, and then other Balu users will see it while they shop.

Balu is about the small, the revolutionary, the transformational. Like the social enterprise that donates all its profits to scholarships for the children of its overseas workforce; or the company that makes products out of waste plastic recovered from our oceans. These are the products you don't often see side-by-side with the global brands. These are the products that Balu wants to promote.

It's not always easy to identify the amazing from the awful, the strivers from the lazy. Here at Balu we are always open to feedback.

Ultimatley, the decision lies with us as consumers. We must buy whatever product we think is best. Hopefully Balu can make it that little bit easier for us.

2. What brands can I recommend?

Anything!

There are so many incredible people out there, doing so much incredible work, there's no way Balu can ever keep on top of them all without your help. If you know of an organisation you think is doing things right, whatever the industry, whatever their size, click on the Balu icon in your browser, click the plus symbol, and tell us about it!

This is your chance to share great brands and product ideas with the rest of the Balu community
